Lentil soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dried lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Rinse the lentils in a fine mesh strainer and set aside.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté for 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for an additional 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. Add the vegetable broth, lentils, bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender and the soup has thickened slightly.
  6. Remove the bay leaf and discard.
  7. Using an immersion blender or transferring the soup to a blender, blend the soup until it reaches your desired consistency.
  8. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
  9. Serve hot with crusty bread and enjoy!
