Veggie lasagna recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups of chopped veggies (such as zucchini, mushrooms, and bell peppers)
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Once they are cooked, drain them and set them aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté the chopped veggies over medium-high heat until they are tender.
  4. Add the jar of pasta sauce to the skillet with the cooked veggies and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the veggie sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the veggie sauce.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture on top of the lasagna noodles.
  9. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  10. Repeat steps 6-9, starting with another layer of veggie sauce, then lasagna noodles, then the remaining ricotta cheese mixture, and finally another layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
