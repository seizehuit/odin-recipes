Jackfruit tacos recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of jackfruit
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Steps:

  1. Drain and rinse the jackfruit, then use your hands or a fork to shred it into small pieces.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the shredded jackfruit, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine, and sauté for 5-7 minutes, until the jackfruit is heated through and slightly crispy.
  4. Heat the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or over an open flame until slightly charred and pliable.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the jackfruit mixture onto each tortilla. Top with diced avocado, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
